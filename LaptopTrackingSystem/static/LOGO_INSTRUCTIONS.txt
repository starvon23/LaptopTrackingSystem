SCHOOL LOGO SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
================================

To display the Masigonational High School logo on the login page:

1. Save your school logo image as: static/logo.png
   (The image you provided showing the torch, book, and "1974" emblem)

2. The logo should be in PNG format for best quality with transparency

3. Recommended size: 500x500 pixels or larger (it will be automatically resized)

4. Once you save the logo.png file in the static folder, refresh your browser
   and the logo will appear on the login page

The login page has been updated with orange/beige colors to match your school's branding:
- Orange gradient background (#e67e22 to #d35400)
- Orange accent colors throughout
- Circular logo display with white background
- Professional and welcoming design

If you don't have the logo file yet, the system will show a broken image icon
until you add the logo.png file to the static folder.
